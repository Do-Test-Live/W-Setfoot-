Questions?

Here are some common questions by other conscious travellers

We vet and select the best certifications to minimise your travel footprint. The accommodations have undergone thorough assessment to not only comply but thrive in measurements of environmental, social and economic sustainability. All listed accommodations are certified by either Global leaders in Sustainable Tourism Certification, including Green Globe , Earth Check and Preferred by Nature

We take an extra mile for you to purchase carbon offset credits once we book through setfoot. Not only are you a responsible traveller who minimises your travel footprint but also contributing to projects that reduce, remove or avoid greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.

We offer the best deals and do not charge any booking commission or fees. We believe that sustainable =/= premium price. Everyone should be able to take part in actions towards a more sustainable future.

Mexico is home to a wide variety of biological diversity across coastlines, rainforests, farms, parks and so much more. Protecting natural environments, wildlife and natural resources when developing and managing tourism activities is of great importance.
